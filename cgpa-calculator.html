🧠 How is CGPA Calculated?

CGPA is the weighted average of your grade points based on credits.

📝 Formula
CGPA = Σ (Credits × Grade Point)Σ Credits
🎯 Example
If you have:
20 Total Credits
160 Total Grade Points

Then: CGPA = 160 ÷ 20 = 8.00
